Geriatric Care

Our geriatric care caters to the health needs of our senior and elderly patients. Some of the geriatric syndromes we manage include dementia, difficulties in walking, frequent falls, memory loss, incontinence and others. All Pioneer Generation (PG) and Community Health Assist Scheme (CHAS) cardholders can enjoy subsidised medical care at all My Family Clinic outlets. Our geriatric care services include, but are not limited to:
  • Chronic disease management
  • Dementia
  • Incontinence
  • Joint and bone care
